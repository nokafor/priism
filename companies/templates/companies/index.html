PRIISM

Princeton Instant Scheduling Machine

PRIISM is an online scheduling tool that allows artistic directors to easily manage the rehearsals for their respective companies. It was created, by artistic directors, for other artistic directors, to alleviate the hassles of scheduling by hand. Currently, the site is protected via Princeton CAS rendering it useful only to Princeton organizations and students. However, it is in the process of being expanded for use by any organization.

How It Works?


PRIISM digitalizes the collection of conflicts and other relevant information for dance companies (e.g. rehearsal times and places, company members, etc). Once added to the system, members can input their conflict information. Then, using the collected information and a matching algorithm, admins for each company can use PRIISM to automatically create schedules that optimize attendance based on the given constraints.
